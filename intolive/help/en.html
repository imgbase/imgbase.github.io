4. Why is there no sound when a lock screen is set?

Unfortunately, when you set a lock screen using Live Photos, it does not emit a sound. The lock screen feature can only be managed by iOS. Thus, a sound feature for the iPhone lock screen cannot be implemented. We hope Apple will support this option in the future.

5. How to upload Live Photos to Facebook

If you want to upload Live Photos to Facebook group and business pages, please use the Facebook app (blue), not the Facebook Page app (orange). When uploading is complete, it will appear as a still image, but it will be Live a few minutes later. (7 - 10 minutes) Facebook needs time for uploading Live Photos to their servers. Just wait a short time, and there should be no problem.

By the way, if your business page has been added to Business Manager (Facebook's advertising platform) you will not be able to add it to the personal profile, and you won‚Äôt be able to see your business page on the Facebook blue app. In this case, you can't upload Live Photos to your FB Page because Facebook does not have any feature developed regarding this function.

6. When Live Photos are played, they expand and my text is cropped.

The Live Photo format expands the photo area by about 8% when played. What is your original video size? When you add text to the frame it's good to place the text about 10% away from the edge of the original video or photo. If your video width is 1000px for example, try adding text 100px away from the edge.

7. When will it be released for Android?

Unfortunately Android does not currently support Live Photos, so we have no plans for developing an Android version of intoLive.
